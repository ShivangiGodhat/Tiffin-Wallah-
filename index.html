About Tiffin Wallah

Our Story, Mission, and Commitment to Quality

Our Mission

At Tiffin Wallah, we believe that everyone deserves to enjoy the warmth and comfort of home-cooked meals, regardless of their busy schedules. Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional home cooking and modern convenience by connecting local home chefs with people who crave authentic, healthy, and delicious food.

We started with a simple idea: what if we could bring the taste of home to your doorstep? Today, we're proud to serve thousands of customers across Mumbai, delivering not just meals, but the love and care that goes into every home-cooked dish.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time do you deliver meals?

We deliver meals between 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM for lunch and 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM for dinner. You can specify your preferred delivery time during checkout.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. For weekly and monthly plans, you can cancel up to 24 hours before the next delivery.

Do you deliver on weekends?

Yes, we deliver 7 days a week including weekends and holidays. You can choose to skip weekends if you prefer.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

We offer various meal options including Jain, no-onion-no-garlic, and custom dietary plans. Please specify your requirements during registration.

How do you ensure food quality and hygiene?

All our partner kitchens are FSSAI certified and regularly inspected. We maintain strict hygiene standards and conduct quality checks before delivery.
